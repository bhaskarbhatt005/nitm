About Us

National Institute of Technology and Management is a registered trade mark division of INDRA GANDHI EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY. Regd. to Govt of NCT Delhi having its Regt. no 3274. This society is registered by Govt. of India under society ACT 1860 under 21 provision. NITM is an ISO 9001-2008 Certified Organization by JAS-ANZ (Australia).We have followed all the quality procedure establish by JAS-ANZ for maintaining the best quality.
