About Us

National Institute of Technology and Management is a registered trade mark division of INDRA GANDHI EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY. Regd. to Govt of NCT Delhi having its Regt. no 3274. This society is registered by Govt. of India under society ACT 1860 under 21 provision. NITM is an ISO 9001-2008 Certified Organization by JAS-ANZ (Australia).We have followed all the quality procedure establish by JAS-ANZ for maintaining the best quality. NITM is also authorized organization for running not only IT Education but vocational education like FD/NTT/NPTT/Cutting & tailoring.



Our Associations & Affiliations

NITM is associated with various reputed universities & Boards. We are Authorized study centers of some of most reputed Indian universities like Karnataka State open University, MS university, EIILM Universities, Agra University, Ch. Charan Singh University, We are also associated to Vishavkarma University of Self employment & Board of secondry Education (Madhya Bharat) Gwalior. Our primary focus has always been on job oriented courses which can enable a person for earn his or her livelihood. To spread the education to a common man we have started many others branches in not only Haryana but in other states also. Our prime object is to develop the skill of a common man to make him self dependent. We always emphasis on quality education so that we can develop a strong nation. To meet this purpose we have tied up with some very reputed universities & educational organization.
