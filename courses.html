Bachelor's degree courses

Regular degree programs in Arts, Science, Commerce, and Social Sciences are three-year programs.

  • Bachelor of Arts

    • Course offered in Literature, Economics, Languages, Philosophy, History, Journalism, Psychology, Political Science, Archaeology, Public Administration, Music, and Fine Arts; also available in Computer Science, Mathematics, Advertising, and Cinema Sciences

  • Bachelor of Science

    • Course offered in Literature, Economics, Languages, Philosophy, History, Journalism, Psychology, Political Science, Archaeology, Public Administration, Music, and Fine Arts; also available in Computer Science, Mathematics, Advertising, and Cinema Sciences

  • Bachelor of Commerce

    • Specializations available in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Geography, Environmental Science, Information Technology, Computer Science, Geology, Agriculture, Mathematics, etc.

  • Bachelor of Engg./Tech

    • Specializations are available such as Computer Applications (also offered exclusively as BCA), Electrical and Electronics, Information Technology, Automobiles, Software, Biotechnology, Civil, and Mechanical
    • Top institutes: IIT-Kanpur, IIT-Delhi, and IIT-Kharagpur; Christ University (Bangalore) is ranked high for Computer Applications

  • BMS/BBA/BBS

    • Specializations include Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, Management of Services, Management of Global Business and Tourism Management.

  • Bachelor of Law

    • Specializations include Criminal Law, Civil Law, Labour Law, International Law
    • The curriculum includes seminars, tutorial work, moot courts, and practical training programs.

  • Bachelor of Medicine (MBBS)

    • The duration between four to five-and-a-half years; the program includes a 12-month long internship

Let’s look at the list of specialized undergraduate/bachelor’s degrees programs offered in India:

  • Bachelor of Arts
  • Bachelor of Science
  • Bachelor of Commerce
  • Bachelor of Engg./Tech BMS/BBA/BBS
  • Bachelor of Law
  • Bachelor of Medicine (MBBS)

Master's degree Courses/ Programe

What is a Master’s Degree?

A master’s degree is what you pursue once you have already finished advanced studies in your field. Basically, opting for a Master’s degree means that you wish to specialise in a specific area of your study. The master’s degree or postgraduate course may be 1 or 2 years long. Some of these courses cost relatively less than the under-graduate courses, except a few like Masters in Business Administration (MBA). Pursuing a Master’s degree involves undergoing in-depth study in your field such that it enables you to demonstrate a high level of expertise in your area of professional practice. Once you are a postgraduate, you possess a high level of knowledge and advanced skills and techniques associated with your area of study. This is why most companies today look to recruit postgraduate students.

Let’s look at the list of Master's degrees programs offered in India:

  • MBA
  • Master Of Human Resource Management [MHRM]
  • Post Graduate Diploma In Management [PGDM]
  • Master In Management Studies [M.M.S]
  • Master Of Personnel Management [MPM]
  • Master In Marketing Management [MMM]
  • Master In International Business [MIB]
  • Master in Commerce (M.Com)
  • Master Of Computer Applications(MCA)
  • Master Of Pharmacy [M.Pharm]
  • Master of Science (M.Sc.)
  • Master of Arts
